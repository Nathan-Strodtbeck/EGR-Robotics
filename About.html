About us

FRC Team #5980

Overview

Our job as a team and as individuals is to work as a whole in creating the most advanced and complex robot possible, while striving for excellence and thinking intuitively, and solving problems in the STEM fields. The 2016 year is our rookie year and we are trying our hardest to make our mark. With help from many mentors and other teams we think that we have what it takes to be stronghold champions.

Join us - New members always welcome

Looking to join the EGR Robotics Team? Great! Robotics meets Tuesdays and Thursdays 7-9pm in the room next to the band room. Look for our members wearing robotics shirts if you have any questions and feel free to contact us through social media or email.

There is a place in robotics for everyone. We have multiple teams including building, electrical, code, CAD, and business. Everyone has a talent that our team can use. That is what makes us one of the best teams around. Stop by anytime and meet the team.
