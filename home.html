A Little About Us

EGR Robotics successfully competed last year as a rookie team, receiving 8 FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) awards. This year, East Grand Rapids recognizes our team as a varsity sport, so we hope to continue to represent our school with a tradition of excellence.

Our Mission Statement

The mission of East Grand Rapids (EGR) Robotics FRC Team 5980 is to inspire students in STEM fields, encourage diversity, and cultivate community outreach, leadership development, and business skills. We seek to engage students in valuable technological and scientific skills needed for the work force; by doing this we create diligent citizens that are prepared for work in a global community. To reach out to our community we plan on holding school assemblies, robot demos, and parades. In addition, we will attempt to make partnerships with diabetes education, Blandford Nature Center, and the Helen Devos Children's Hospital. To mold future leaders, opportunities are given to the members of EGR Robotics to create, build, plan, and develop in many different areas, such as sponsorship, coding, building of the robot and robot prototypes, and maintaining of social media platforms. To emphasize our motto that it’s “not just about the robot,” we also make Business a priority by engaging the whole team on creating this Business plan. We are constantly re-evaluating our goals and adapting to change as it comes in order to create a team which learns and grows better year by year.

Help Wanted

EGR Robotics is fortunate to have great leadership, from both students and mentors. We work together but also divide and conquer with different subsections including a drive team, pit crew, coding, mechanical, marketing, and business, so everyone can get involved in various aspects of our team. Please let us know if you want to contribute or join!
